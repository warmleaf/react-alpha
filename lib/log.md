# log

> Can not be used nested

+ **\***     - italic
+ **\_**     - bold
+ **\-**     - bold & italic
+ **\<B\>**  - blue
+ **\<C\>**  - cyan
+ **\<G\>**  - green
+ **\<R\>**  - rad
+ **\<Y\>**  - yellow
+ **\<*B\>** - blue & italic
+ **\<*C\>** - cyan & italic
+ **\<*G\>** - green & italic
+ **\<*R\>** - rad & italic
+ **\<*Y\>** - yellow & italic
+ **\<_B\>** - blue & bold
+ **\<_C\>** - cyan & bold
+ **\<_G\>** - green & bold
+ **\<_R\>** - rad & bold
+ **\<_Y\>** - yellow & bold
+ **\<-B\>** - blue & bold & italic
+ **\<-C\>** - cyan & bold & italic
+ **\<-G\>** - green & bold & italic
+ **\<-R\>** - rad & bold & italic
+ **\<-Y\>** - yellow & bold & italic
